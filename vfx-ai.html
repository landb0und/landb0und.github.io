CGI · Compositing · AI

CGI

Anything that wasn't on set, built in 3D and dropped in — creatures, vehicles, props, full environments and set extensions, plus simulated fire, water, smoke and destruction. Modeled, lit, rendered, then comped into the plate.

Compositing

Combining and cleaning real footage so the seams vanish — wire and rig removal, sky and background replacement, screen inserts, green-screen keys, multi-plate blends, and color/grain matching. The shot was captured; comp makes it read.

What AI Can Do

Accelerates the grunt work and opens options — rotoscoping, upscaling, denoise, gen-fill cleanup, style frames and previs, and generative plates or elements when a real shoot isn't feasible. A tool in the pipeline, not the whole pipeline.
