09. Am I The Right Fit? Straight answer, both ways
✓Hire me when
  • You want one person who can direct, shoot, cut, and finish — instead of stitching together four vendors.
  • You need an embedded creative who treats your channel like their own, not a contractor checking boxes.
  • The timeline is tight and the budget is real, and you'd rather have taste than a render farm.
  • You're scaling content and need someone who's built the operation before.
✕I'm probably not it when
  • You need a 60-person VFX house for a tentpole studio feature — I lead and supe, I'm not a vendor at that scale.
  • You want the lowest bid with no creative input. That's not the work I do well.
  • You need broadcast news or corporate AV ops. Not my lane.
  • The brief is "just make it go viral" with nothing behind it. I build real things that happen to travel.
